Animated Cartoons

Award-winning educational cartoons I animated and directed for Northeast Iowa RC&D. It includes a five-part recycling series following five different anthropomorphic characters on their recycling journey, and a cartoon using professional wrestling to comedically communicate how we can use soil retention practices to battle increased flooding.
Stories written by Rod Marlatt Animation / character design - Hans Larson Background design / character design - Austin Feuerhelm Sponsors: University of Iowa, Turkey River Watershed Management Authority, Fayette County Conservation Board (Iowa), and Fayette County Solid Waste Commission (Iowa)
